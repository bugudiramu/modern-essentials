# GEMINI.md - Shared Utilities

## Overview
Shared utility functions for currency, dates, and common logic.

## Key Utilities
- **Currency:** Helpers for formatting and calculating prices (handling Paise/Rupees).
- **Date:** Helpers for subscription scheduling (next delivery date, frequency calculations).
- **Validation:** Common regex or validation helpers.

## Conventions
- **Pure Functions:** Utilities should be pure and side-effect free whenever possible.
- **Unit Tested:** Every utility should have corresponding tests.
